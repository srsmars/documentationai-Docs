## Overview

Set up your MOHD SHABAN documentation space quickly. Follow these steps to create an account, launch a new space, and publish your first page. You complete the process in under five minutes.

<Callout kind="info">

MOHD SHABAN uses MDX for rich documentation with components like Steps and Cards.

</Callout>

## Step-by-Step Setup

Use the guided process below to get running.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account" icon="user-plus">

Sign up using your preferred method. MOHD SHABAN supports multiple authentication options.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">

Visit `https://app.mohdshaban.com/signup` and enter your email address. Verify via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="GitHub" icon="github">

Click the GitHub button on the sign-up page. Authorize MOHD SHABAN to access your repositories for seamless integration.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a Documentation Space" icon="folder-plus">

From the dashboard, select **New Space**. Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and choose a slug such as `my-project`.

<Callout kind="tip">

Pick a descriptive slug â€“ it forms your space URL: `https://mohdshaban.com/my-project`.

</Callout>

Click **Create Space** to generate your starter template.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Add and Edit Your First Page" icon="edit-3">

Navigate to the root directory and select **New Page**. Name it `introduction.mdx`.

Replace the default content with your own. Use MDX components for interactive docs.

<CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
  ```mdx
---
title: Introduction
description: Welcome to my project.
---

## Hello World

This is your first **MDX** page.

<Callout kind="success">Success!</Callout>
  ```
  ```markdown
# Introduction (Markdown only)

Welcome to my project.

**Note:** MDX supports JSX components.
  ```
</CodeGroup>

Preview changes in real-time, then click **Publish** to go live.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Page not publishing?">
    Check for syntax errors in MDX. Wrap special characters like `{variable}` or `<Component>` in backticks. Ensure frontmatter has no blank lines.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Slow previews?">
    Large images or complex components increase load time. Optimize assets and limit nested elements.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
